Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

How many passengers can travel?

Our vehicle comfortably seats up to 4 passengers — including children and infants — in addition to your driver. For larger groups, please call us on 0449 188 872 to discuss your options.

Can I bring luggage?

Yes — we can fit up to 4 standard checked suitcases, plus carry-on items. Travelling with more, or with oversized items? Call us on 0449 188 872 before booking and we'll confirm we can fit everything in.

Are baby seats available?

Yes — baby and child seats are available on request at no extra charge. Tick the box when booking and note the child's age so we fit the right one.

What vehicle do you use?

A Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV GSR in Graphite Grey — a premium plug-in hybrid SUV with leather interior, dual-zone climate control and a 5-star safety rating. It's the only vehicle we operate, so you always know exactly what's arriving.

What condition is the car in?

It's a late-model Outlander PHEV, maintained to dealership standard, professionally detailed, and cleaned before every transfer.

How do I book?

Fill in the short form above — it takes under two minutes. We'll reply with your fixed fare and confirm the pickup, usually within the hour. Prefer to talk? Call or WhatsApp 0449 188 872.
